Team Potter

Zachary Latta, Margaret Knoblock, Kaitlyn Bishay, and Muhammad Hafez
The Problem:

Mattel is trying to create a new line of toy trains that will encompass a wide variety of interests. They are looking for teams to submit design proposals for a train that will focus on a specific population. Each train must contain two cars that can attach to the tracks in the Miniature Train project.

Brainstorming:

Doctor Who?
Sherlock?
Harry Potter?
Lord of the Rings?
Nerdfighteria?
Avengers?

We decided on Harry Potter because of the large potential audience and room it gave us for creativity.

Ideas:
  • First car is Dumbledore.
  • Each car represents a different house.
  • Follows architecture style of Hogwarts.
Shared parts:
  • Pegs
  • Wheels
  • Linkage arms
  • Train body base
Constraints:
  • Bottom of each car must be 5.5" by 2"
  • The car must fit on the track from the Miniature Train project.
